SunBriteTV All-Weather Outdoor TVs

Turn your backyard into a luxurious year-round retreat with SunBriteTV All-Weather TVs. Built for permanent outdoor installations, SunBrite outdoor televisions feature a weather-proof housing to protect it from the seasons, and an extra-bright LED screen for a vividly clear picture day or night.

SunBrite TVs come in 3 distinct series; each uniquely designed for specific outdoor locations, light conditions, and outdoor activities. Choose the Veranda Series if you’re using in low activity, fully-shaded locations such as a covered patio or screened porch. For higher activity areas with partial sun such as a canopied deck or under a pergola, the Signature Series is the ideal choice. For areas that are most active and receive direct sunlight, such as poolside or bar side, select the Pro Series for its extremely bright screen and commercial-grade durability.
