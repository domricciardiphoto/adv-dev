FREE SHIPPING:
Free shipping on orders $49 and up*! Get free standard or economy shipping on all parcel shippable orders $49 and up.
* Excludes major appliances, select televisions, mattresses, barbeque grills, video game consoles, items displaying "in-store only" messaging, and all other items not qualified for parcel shipping. Gift cards, excluded products and taxes do not count toward the $49 order total. If a coupon lowers the total purchase price to less than $49, you will be charged for all shipping costs. Please be advised that any orders that qualify for free shipping will not include free haul away service. Please contact customer service for our installation and haul away options.
NEXT DAY DELIVERY:
Next Day Delivery is available in the local P.C. Richard & Son Delivery Area on all in-stock TVs and major appliances when ordered before 5pm, except as noted below:
New York and New Jersey P.C. Richard & Son delivery service is not available on Monday.
Connecticut - P.C. Richard & Son delivery service is not available on Wednesday or Sunday.
SHIPPING INSIDE THE P.C. RICHARD & SON LOCAL DELIVERY AREA:
All Major Appliances**
$49.80 Flat Rate includes delivery, unpacking, and basic setup. Each additional appliance will be charged $14 for delivery. Please call (877) 727-1909 for additional installation options. Manhattan deliveries will incur a $10 surcharge.
** Appliances include but are not limited to: refrigerators, ranges, cook-tops, wall ovens, range hoods, dishwashers, microwaves, freezers, washers, dryers, etc.
TVs
Free Shipping on select TVs in the P.C. Richard & Son Delivery Area includes delivery and inspection of product.
$49 White Glove Delivery (except Manhattan) includes delivery, uncrate, inspect and test TV; assembly of TV onto the manufacturers supplied pedestal; and connecting TV to 1 existing video source and power outlet. (Manhattan - $10 surcharge). Please contact customer service after you place your order if you wish to include white glove delivery.
Mattresses
$49.80 Flat Rate includes delivery, unpacking, setup and haul-away of your old mattress. Each additional mattress will be charged $14 for delivery. Manhattan deliveries will incur a $10 surcharge.
Upholstered Beds & Headboards
Free delivery. Upholstered beds and headboards are made to order and ship directly from the manufacturer. Delivery time can take 3-4 weeks.
Furniture
Freight Shipments - $49.80 In-Home Delivery product is brought to the room of your choice.
Select furniture is delivered via parcel service. As such, they are subject to standard parcel rates and service options.
Barbeque Grills
$49 Flat Rate shipping per item on Barbeque Grills.
Outdoor/Patio Furniture
Free Curbside Delivery product is delivered to the edge of driveway.
Wall Beds
Free Delivery - A signature is required for all deliveries. Carrier will call to set up a delivery time. Your item(s) will be delivered to the first dry interior area available.
  This service does not include set up or assembly of items or removal of packaging materials.
  Installation and upgraded delivery is available in select areas.
  Please call Leto Muro at 844-298-2531 to see if installation or upgraded delivery methods are available in your area.
Fitness/Exercise Equipment
Free Threshold Delivery. A signature is required for all deliveries. Fitness products ship direct from the manufacturer. Delivery can take 10-14 business days. Large or oversized items will be delivered by common carrier. Your item(s) will be delivered to the first dry interior area available, inside your front door or garage. Delivery does not include set up or assembly of items or removal of packaging materials.
Carrier will call or email to set up a delivery appointment. Please include a daytime phone number in the shipping information at the time of check out. Deliveries are made between the hours of 8 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday, and will be scheduled as a 4 hour window.
White Glove Assembly is available on select Proform branded fitness equipment.
Hiring a pro will have the right tools and experience to do the assembly as quickly and safely as possible, so you can focus on enjoying your new gear, instead of assembling it.
Once you have an accurate delivery date on the arrival of your equipment, please call ICON Health & Fitness at 1-800-445-2480 to make an assembly appointment. Applicable charge for white glove assembly: $169 for assembly of 1 piece of fitness equipment per product instructions.
Typical assembly time of 1-2 hours. Moving equipment to room is not included.
Recycling Program
P.C. Richard & Son offers a recycling program in the states of New York, New Jersey, Connecticut & Pennsylvania for customers purchasing a replacement appliance from P.C. Richard & Son. The replaced appliances are sent to a licensed third part recycling company who ensures that the products will be recycled in accordance with all applicable laws.
SHIPPING OUTSIDE THE P.C. RICHARD & SON LOCAL DELIVERY AREA:
We ship within the contiguous United States and do not ship internationally. While we take all possible measures to ensure that your order leaves our warehouse in superior condition, damages may occur to items during transit. It is extremely important that you open and inspect your merchandise immediately upon arrival from Pilot Air, our freight shipping partner for orders outside of our delivery area. Damages must be reported to them within 3 business days of your delivery for them to honor and investigate the claim. You can begin the damage claim process here. Please allow 15 calendar days after your claim for the investigation to be completed.

In-Store Pick Up:
Choose our In-Store Pickup option when adding a product to your shopping cart or during the checkout process. During checkout you will be prompted to select a primary showroom location followed up by up to 5 more secondary showrooms based on the zip code you have entered.

Please Note:

For your security, the person picking up an order in store must be the person named on the purchasing credit card. Only the cardholder or an authorized user on the credit card account may pick up items in store. An authorized cardholder is a cardholder with the same account number as the credit card used for the purchase and has the same last name as the cardholder.

The following is required for all In-Store Pickups:

1. A state issued photo ID or driver's license in the name of the purchaser.
2. The credit card used for the purchase.
3. Your e-mail confirmation or order number.

The dollar amount of your order will be withheld from your available credit card balance until the order has been completed. NOTE: Do not go to the store to pick up the items until you receive a "Merchandise Ready" e-mail from us. If you have any questions or concerns, contact us at (877) 727-1909 or via e-mail.
