Frequently Asked Questions...
What is the P.C. Richard & Son Credit Card?
P.C. Richard & Son offers customers a credit card through Synchrony Bank, with a revolving credit line to use again and again at P.C. Richard & Son locations.
How do I get a P.C. Richard & Son Credit Card?
You can apply for a P.C. Richard & Son Credit Card* online here or in-store. Click here to find the location nearest you.
Where do I check the status of my account?
To check the status of your P.C. Richard & Son Credit Card click on the Manage My Account link or you may go to Synchrony Financial’s website by clicking here.
After logging into your account, you will be able to:

        • Make a payment
        • View your balance
        • Update your account information
        • Schedule up to your next 12 payments
How can I make a payment on my P.C. Richard & Son Credit Card?
P.C. Richard & Son offers 4 convenient ways to pay your P.C. Richard & Son Credit Card bill.
• Pay Online: Click here to manage your account and make payments online.
• Pay In-store: Bring your P.C. Richard & Son credit card statement into a P.C. Richard & Son location and pay your bill by cash or check.
• Pay by Phone: Call Synchrony Bank Customer Service at 1-866-396-8254.
• Pay by Mail: Mail payments to the address below. Ensure that you have plenty of time for your payment to arrive prior to due date.
                         • Synchrony Bank, PO Box 960061, Orlando, FL 32896 - 0061
